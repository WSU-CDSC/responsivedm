About the CSEJ Collection

In 2011, the Center for Social and Environmental Justice (CSEJ) at WSU Vancouver and the WSU Vancouver Library participated in a one year project that involved hosting a digital collection from local organizations advocating social and environmental justice. This collection was established for the purposes of research and entails records from the Clark County Council for the Homeless, including documents and data spreadsheets that concentrate on local homelessness from the late 1990s to 2011. The collection uses PDF and XLS formats. Free Adobe and Excel spreadsheet readers can be found online. This digital collection is managed by the library, but the collection itself is not a part of the Archives and Special Collections. Questions pertaining to the materials hosted in this digital collection should be directed the Clark County Council for the Homeless
