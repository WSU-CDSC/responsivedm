Welcome to Mapping Clark County

Welcome to the Clark County Historical Museum's Mapping Clark County Collection, a collaborative effort between the Clark County Historical Museum (CCHM), the Washington State University Vancouver Library. The digital collection includes selections from the Clark County Historical Museum's maps collection, aerial photographs collection, general photograph collection and land plats. Images include subject sections involving geography, aerial views, street scenes and transportation. The collection is intended to show examples of Clark County's transforming landscape over time. This digital collection was made possible through the funding from the Historical Promotion Grants Program.
